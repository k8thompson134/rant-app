# How to Install RantTrack (APK) - Easy Guide

## Before You Start

You'll need:
- An Android phone
- The APK file (I'll send you a link to download it)
- About 5 minutes of your time

---

## Step 1: Download the APK

1. Click the link I sent you
2. Tap **Download** (it will download to your phone's "Downloads" folder)
3. Wait for it to finish (you'll see "Download complete")

---

## Step 2: Allow Installation from Unknown Apps

Your phone might warn you about installing apps from unknown sources. This is normal!

**On your phone:**
1. Open **Settings**
2. Tap **Apps** (or **Applications**)
3. Tap the **menu icon** (three dots in top right)
4. Choose **Special app access** or **Install unknown apps**
5. Find your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Samsung Internet, etc.)
6. Toggle **"Allow from this source"** to ON (it will turn blue/green)

**Done!** You only need to do this once.

---

## Step 3: Install RantTrack

1. Open your **Downloads** folder (usually at the bottom of your home screen)
2. Find the file that says **"rantapp"** or **"ranttrack"**
3. Tap on it
4. A screen will appear asking to install
5. Tap **Install**
6. Wait for it to finish (usually 30 seconds to 1 minute)
7. Tap **Open** when it's done!
